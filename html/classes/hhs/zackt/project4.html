Julio Jones

by Zack Tovey


High School and College

Julio Jones went to High School at Foley High School in Foley, Alabama, then went on to play at the University of Alabama. He was a state champ track-star and was recognized by multiple selections. He recorded over 1,000 recieving yards and 16 touchdowns in his Junior year. In college, he was a standout athlete and was noticed by multiple NFL agents and was drafted to Atlanta in the 1st round.

Day in a life of an NFL Star

Julio Jones plays in Atlanta, for the Falcons. He's the #1 wide reciever in Atlanta, and is said to be the 2nd best in the NFL. He has a good relationship with his team and family. This year, he hopes to win Atlanta's first superbowl. So far this year, he has 9 catches for 172 yards, with 2 touchdowns.
