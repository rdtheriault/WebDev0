About them

Snow Tha Product, actual name Claudia Alexandra Feliciano, is a Mexican hip hop artist who made something of herself, with the help of her family and true friends aka the Woke Crew. Snow got the idea for her name from the Disney princess snow white, so her stage name was originally Snow white the product, until Disney claimed it was copyright so she had to change it. Snow tha product has her own website, where she sells clothing and such, you can click here to go on it.

Their Career

In 2013 she released Good nights & Bad mornings, which helped her catch the attention of 3 record labels, in which she ended up signing with Atlantic Records.
