Biography

Carli Anne Lloyd was born in July 16, 1982 in a small town at the time called Derlan, New Jersey. She started playing soccer at age 5. She developed her skills by practicing for several of hours on her local field. However when she entered high school she was named twice the High School Player of the Year for her outstanding skills in soccer. After she graduated high school she wanted to go to a college were she was close by her family, so she decided to go to Rutgers University. She was also an all-star there as well.

Lloyd's started in the U.S Junior national team. However there was a point she wanted to quit the team due to being cut off the team. Lloyd made her debut in 2005 when she was playing for the U.S senior team against Ukraine. In the 2008 and 2012 olympics she scored the winning goals. Her jersey number is number 10. Carli Lloyd is the first woman to score a hat trick at the world cup.In 2015 Carli Lloyd earned The Golden Ball Trophy in the 2015 World Cup.Another fact about Carli is that her annual salary is 70,000 dollars a year.
