Nissan S14 silvia

The S14 Silvia is a rear wheel drive coupe that was first introduced in japan 1994. The silvia had a
softer suspension and no rear sway bar. In 1996 the came out with a different style of the car which had a different front-end.
This was known as the kouki front-end as the other was known as the zenki.
