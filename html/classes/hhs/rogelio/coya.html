About Toby

Toby Flenderson is the human resources representative for the Scranton branch of paper distributor Dunder Mifflin/Sabre.

More Toby

Mild-mannered to the point of being physically incapable to voice his opinions or assert himself, Toby can be somewhat mournful about his life choices (he fell into the field of Human Resources after leaving his training at seminary in order to pursue the woman he'd later marry and painfully divorce).

  • Occupation: Human Resources Representative, Dunder Mifflin, Scranton
  • Family: Unnamed parents (divorced) Rory (brother)
  • Spouse(s): Kathy Becker (ex-wife)
  • Children: Sasha Flenderson (daughter)
